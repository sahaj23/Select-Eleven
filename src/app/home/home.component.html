Indian Cricket Team BCCI

About

The India men's national cricket team, also known as Team India and Men in Blue, is governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI), and is a Full Member of the International Cricket Council (ICC) with Test, One-Day International (ODI) and Twenty20 International (T20I) status. Sport: Test cricket League: International Cricket Council Championships: 2011 Cricket World Cup

Major Tournaments Won

  • 1983 ODI World Cup
  • 2007 T20 World Cup
  • 2011 ODI World Cup
  • 2013 Champions Trophy
